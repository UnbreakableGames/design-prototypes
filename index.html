Core Loop

Mine Asteroid → Collect Ore → Refine into Bar → Spend Bars on Upgrades → Repeat

Tiers 4-7 have enemies (abstracted as danger chance per mining hit). Combat upgrades reduce danger. Death = lose carried ore.

Multi-Bar Costs

Early upgrades cost 1 bar type. Mid upgrades cost 2 types. Late upgrades cost 3 types. This keeps all tiers of mining relevant.

Key Buildings

Landing Pad, Refiners (x6), Refiner Speed, Thruster Dock, Drone Bay (auto-mines Tier 1-3), Auto-Collector (auto-collects bars), Armory (unlocks combat upgrades).
