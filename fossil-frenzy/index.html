How to Play

1
Dig — Click dirt blocks in the dig site to excavate. You can only dig a block if the one above it is cleared.
2
Find — Unknown artifacts appear as mystery boxes. Click one to select it.
3
Clean — Click "Move to Cleaner", then click the item repeatedly to brush off the dirt and reveal what it is.
4
Research — Cleaned items auto-place on a research table. Research takes real time. Come back later to collect.
5
Upgrade — Spend Research Grants (RG) on upgrades at the bottom. Deeper layers and higher rarities await.
Rare items come in pieces. Collect all fragments to assemble them! Dig site resets every 5 minutes.
